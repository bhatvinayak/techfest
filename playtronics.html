Playtronics/Playtricals

• A team consists of max 3+1 persons
• Requires basic knowledge of fundamentals on basic circuit theory
• Hands-on on rigging up the circuits using RLC and other devices such as CRO, transistors and Opamp
• The event will run on 2 rounds
• If the 1ST YEAR Students wants to participate then they have to at least have one 3rd year or final year student in the team.
• The registered team must be present for the event, no swapping of the team members.
Round 1:
ELIMINATION ROUND:
• Basic circuits will be shown. The team has to find the objective or show the working of the circuit as asked by the jury.
• Or some basic applications shall be asked for the circuit shown
ROUND 2:
FUN TIME
• A design shall be given to the team. The team has to rig up the circuit and the output using the components required for the design
• Jury decision is final
