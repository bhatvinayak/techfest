4 Pics 1 word

General rules
 4 Pics1Word's gameplay is very simple PUZZLE game.
 Each level displays four pictures linked by one word-the player's aim is to workout what the wordis?
 A team consists of max 3 persons
 Requires BASIC FUNDAMENTALS OF ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS Knowledge
ELIMINATION ROUND:
 SURPRIZE fungame
Final round
Buzzer Round
-A 4 PIC figure will be displayed & the team who presses the buzzer first gets to answer first –5 figures
 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5marks for the wrong answer or if team doesn’t after they pressBuzzers.–No forwarding
 Answering time is only 30seconds
 The team has to justify for the answer if asked by the Jury.Note jury decision is final.
