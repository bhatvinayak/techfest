CRYPTO-HUNT

Crypto-hunt is an event inspired by the traditional Treasure Hunt but involving concepts of technology. The clues are hidden offline in the Sai Vidya campus as well as online on the internet. Participants are supposed to follow the clues and reach the treasure.

Registration Fee: 100/- per team

Rules and Regulations:

Teams of 3 participants each.

Duration is 90 minutes.

The first clue will be given, students must follow the clues and reach the treasure which will be a part of the clues.

Use of mobile phones and computers is allowed. It is preferred that the participants have a smartphone with internet access and camera functionality.

The coordinators can be contacted in case of any issues.

The coordinators will not help regarding clues.

Tampering the physical clues hidden offline will lead to immediate disqualification from the event.
