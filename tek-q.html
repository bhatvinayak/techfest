TEK-Q

Number of participants for each team is two members.
Usage of mobiles or electronic gadgets shall not be entertained.
If anyone is caught using mobile or trying to cheat, then that team will be eliminated on the spot.
TEK-Q consists of 5 rounds
The first round is prelims, it consists of 15 questions, 10 technical and 5 G.K.
The time allotted for the first round is 18 minutes. Number of teams for the second round will be based on the total number of participants.
Approximately 60% of the total number of participants. Each team will be asked 2 questions (1 technical and 1 G.K) in the second round.
Answers for each technical and G.K question will be awarded with 5 points and 3 points each. Questions unanswered will be passed to the other teams, teams answering the passed questions will be awarded 2 points and 1 point each for technical and G.K question. Seven teams will be selected for the next round.
In the third round (Connect the pics)
Five teams will be selected for the next round.
The fourth round is a Buzzer round.
A total of 5 questions will be displayed and the clues for the questions will be disclosed in the form of images. Each question will consist of 4 clues. Points will be awarded to the teams answering the question based on the number of clues used by each team for answering.
(5 points for using only 1 clue, 3 points for using 2 clues, 2 points for using 2 clues and only 1 point for using 1 clue). Three teams will be selected for the final round The fifth and final round is the lightning round.
30 seconds all the questions they can answer.
